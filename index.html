Privacy Policy

Last updated: September 24, 2025

Motivify is a simple motivational quotes app. We respect your privacy and this page explains how information is handled within the app.

Information Collection

Motivify does not collect, store, or share any personal information. The app works fully offline and does not require you to provide any data.

Local Data

Quotes are stored within the app itself. This data remains on your device and is never sent to external servers.

Third-Party Services

This version of Motivify does not use analytics, advertising, or third-party SDKs. If future updates include such services, this policy will be updated accordingly.

Changes

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Changes will be posted on this page with the updated date above.
