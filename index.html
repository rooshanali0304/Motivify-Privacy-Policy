Privacy Policy for Motivify

Last updated: September 24, 2025

Motivify ("we", "us", or "our") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how Motivify handles information when you use our mobile application.

1. Information We Do NOT Collect

Motivify is a simple quotes app and does not collect or transmit any personal data by default. We do not require sign-in, and no personal user data (name, email, device identifiers) is collected or stored by the app in this initial release.

2. Local Storage

The app stores quote text locally on your device for offline access. This data remains on your device and is not uploaded to any server.

3. Third-Party Services

We currently do not integrate third-party analytics, advertising, or cloud services. If we add third-party SDKs (analytics, ads, cloud sync) in future updates, we will update this policy and clearly list those services and their data practices.

4. Ads & Monetization

This release contains no ads. If ads are added later, we will disclose the ad provider(s) and update this policy.

5. Your Rights

Because we currently do not collect personal data, there is no data to access or delete. If this changes in future versions, you will have the right to request access, correction, or deletion of your personal data as required by applicable law.

6. Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy from time to time. We will post the updated date at the top of this page. Material changes will be communicated via the app’s Play Store listing.
